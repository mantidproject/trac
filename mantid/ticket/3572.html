Ability to flip the unrolled instrument view

Reported by: Stuart Campbell Owned by: Roman Tolchenov
Priority: major Milestone: Release 2.0
Component: MantidPlot Keywords:
Cc: Blocked By:
Blocking: Tester: Michael Reuter

Description

For the case of indirect geometry instruments it would be useful to have the option of viewing the unrolled detector view from the 'other side'. What I mean by this is that the neutrons hit the detector from the side that is furthest away from the sample. So the view wants to be looking towards the origin/sample rather than from it.
