Update IntegratePeakTimeSlices to better deal with weaker and edge peaks

Reported by: Ruth Mikkelson Owned by: Ruth Mikkelson
Priority: minor Milestone: Release 2.4
Component: Mantid Keywords:
Cc: Blocked By:
Blocking: Tester:

Description

The current form has been specifically tweaked to deal well with strong peaks.

The update will now accept weaker time slices and also attempt to merge bad time slices with an adjacent good time slice.

The intensity and errors for edge peaks should be a combination of the Fitted values and the actual sample values.
