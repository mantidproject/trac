Muon: Apply period substraction after analysis method

Reported by: Anders Markvardsen Owned by: Anders Markvardsen
Priority: critical Milestone: Release 2.3
Component: MantidPlot Keywords:
Cc: Blocked By: #6002
Blocking: Tester: Nick Draper

Description

At the moment when plotting e.g. a pair on period data where e.g. period 2 is substracted by period 1 the steps are:

  1. substract 2-1
  2. apply asymmetry algorithm

As just discussed with Steve this has a side effect for this particular cause. A proposed way around this is to swap the order of 1. and 2. above.
