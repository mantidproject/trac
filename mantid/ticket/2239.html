comment:2 Changed 10 years ago by Nick Draper

This is more important as we want the load algorithm to be widely used, and due to the size of some files Load will probably be the most cancelled algorithm ever.

comment:3 Changed 10 years ago by Nick Draper

This change is being implemented as a general solution for all algorithms.

All algorithms will:

  1. maintain a vector of weak pointers to any subalgorithms they create. Weak pointers will not prevent the algorithms from being destructed.
  2. On receiving a cancel command will pass it on to any valid sub algorithms.
  3. delete the list of weak pointers when the algorithm is destroyed. As they are weak pointers they have no effect of the lifetime of and subalgorithms.
