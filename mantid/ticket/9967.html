That's the correct behavior. The splatter plot works on having boxes with lots of events where a MDEventWorkspace rebinned to MDHistoWorkspace only has one point per box. Therefore, MDHWs aren't very useful to look at unless you rebin so fine as to get a similar spatial resolution as the MDEventWorkspace implies. Also, the N3 example does not have you rebin the data (N3 step 1 is steps 1 and 2 from N1 and neither involve rebinning), so there shouldn't have been any "problem".
