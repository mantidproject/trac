comment:7 Changed 7 years ago by Ricardo Leal

After discussion with scientists, we realised that the initial approach was not the best.

A new algorithm has been created DetectorEfficiencyCorUser witch parses 2 equations from the IDF and corrects for detector effciciency.

The equations are formula_eff0 and formula_eff.

For more details about this equations, please refer to: INX manual (​http://www.ill.eu/?id=1660)

INX is a data reduction program (written in Fortran) for time-of-flight IN4, IN5 and IN6) data. It allows data grouping, conversion from time to energy, normalisations, absorption correction, etc.
