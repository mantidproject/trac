  1. open the instrument view

1, go to the pick tab

  1. use the Pixel selection tool
  2. move the mouse over some pixels, in the selection box to the right you should get info about the parameters.
  3. The info is listed alphabetically, but grouped by the inst cmpt it comes from, moving from the selected object to the instrument.
  4. To see more, maximise the window, and you can get to the scroll bar by right clicking and moving over while the context menu is visible.
  5. The bank with the most info at the most levels is the middle top of the higher 3, bank B1.
  6. The Add a single crystal peak tool is nicer with this, you just left click on the pixel you want to see.
  7. The Tube/Bank selection tool display as for one of the pixels selected, consistent with the rest of the information.
  8. The shape tools should report nothing
  9. The non detector components do not display anything as at the moment they are not pickable.
