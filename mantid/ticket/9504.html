Make the section selectors more distinguishable

Reported by: Arturs Bekasovs Owned by: Raquel Alvarez Banos
Priority: minor Milestone: Backlog
Component: Muon Keywords: ALC
Cc: Blocked By:
Blocking: Tester: Dan Nixon

Description (last modified by Arturs Bekasovs) (diff)

At the moment, all the section selector lines look the same, and it's quite difficult to understand which of them belong together and which section they correspond to.

A few things to do for improving that:

  • When moving a selector line, in the section table highlight the section row which the selector corresponds to. This is easy.
  • Color selectors differently. Make sure colors are distinguishable - not yellow vs light yellow.
  • Add a new section selector outside the previous one, so they don't get intersected that much.
