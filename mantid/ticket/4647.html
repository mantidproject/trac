Description (last modified by Janik Zikovsky) (diff)

Refs #996, #812 

If your algorithm does NOT inherit from PairedGroupAlgorithm, and gets these inputs:

Input1 : groupA

Input1 : A1

Input2 : A2

For example, try IntegratePeaksMD with two groups.
