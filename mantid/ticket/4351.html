Opened 9 years ago

Last modified 5 years ago

GroupDetectors should average by the number of spectra not detectors in the new group

Reported by: Martyn Gigg Owned by: Martyn Gigg
Priority: major Milestone: Release 2.0
Component: Mantid Keywords:
Cc: Blocked By:
Blocking: Tester: Peter Peterson

Description

GroupDetectors currently averages by the number of detectors in the newly created group. On instruments with hardware grouping this can lead to inconsistent averaging. This was noticed on MERLIN where their short tubes have an 8:1 mapping at the hardware level but the long tubes have a 4:1. When running GroupDetectors2 in averaging mode with a 1:1 map file you get an odd area of lower intensity with the short tubes after running GroupDetectors. See picture
